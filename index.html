Flagship · Early Beta

ProxNet

A smart attendance and access platform using BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) and Face ID verification. BLE detects proximity and presence; face recognition confirms identity. Contactless, automated, and designed to prevent proxy attendance and time fraud.

  • → BLE-based automatic check-in
  • → Face recognition for authentication
  • → Real-time attendance & fraud prevention
  • → Scalable cloud dashboard, mobile-first
